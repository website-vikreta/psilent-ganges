About Us

Psilent Ganges Engineering is a consultancy firm dealing with PSU and Private Sector works in Refinery, Petrochemical, Fertilizer, Chemical and Infrastructure works. We offer a wide range of solutions and curate them to detail, benefitting our clients in their operations.

We have acute experience of more than 34 years in the field of construction and project management, refinery and petrochemicals, Fertilizers, Chemical, Oil and Gas, Cross country pipelines, Cryogenic tankages and API Storage tankages.
