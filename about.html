About Us

Psilent Ganges is an engineering management and solutions firm founded by industry experts with over 37 years of experience witnessing phenomenal evolution in the last 4 decades in the methods of implementing projects, time-line, safety and quality requirement, client's expectation and the contract management during the execution.

"Psilent Ganges" is a one stop solution to all the requirements of our domestic as well as global customers during the proposal, engineering, constructability review and recommendation, execution, project management, contract management and the project closure phase of the project.

"Psilent Ganges" brings expert engineering, planning, design, management and consultancy skills to a wide range of construction and Project management solutions.

At "Psilent Ganges" we shall be committed to provide a solution and generate measurable success for our client with targeted objectives.
