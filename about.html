About Us

We provide a wide range of engineering solutions consisting of project and construction management, with the capability to take your projects from concept to completion considering your unique requirements.

Psilent Ganges brings expert engineering, planning, design, management and consultancy skills to a wide range of construction and Project management solutions, with office in Thane, Maharashtra.

Why Psilent ganges

We provide expert consultancy, design, engineering and management for a wide range of construction and project management in the Refinery, Petrochemical and Infrastructure sector.

We have experience in doing projects covering both design, construction, supervision and term maintenance of infrastructure. We provide,

  • Project Planning & Proposal
  • Project Management Support
  • Site Supervision
  • Project Planner
  • Document Control

Psilent Ganges exists to design and build sustainable and robust engineering solutions which have positive economic, social and environmental impacts whilst embracing the core principles of Innovation, Sustainability and Social Responsibility. We are,

  • Flexible and collaborative approach
  • Socially responsible project delivery
  • Bringing the latest innovation to market
  • Client engagement is the key to success
  • Strong supply chain of industry specialists
  • Extensive industry experience and knowledge

Core Values

Psilent Ganges Culture

Science does not drive humanities. It is humanities that drive science. At Psilent Ganges, we emphasize on having superior ideals and values that align with evolving and revolutionizing the society. We only strive and work to uphold these values. It is the very basis of why we function. We understand that it is our duty to merge these values into our working environment.

Tempering our core principle of dutiful service with absolute integrity, responsibility and reliability is our sole competence.

With a noble mission to amplify the lives of people, community and civilisation; we help to formulate end-to-end solutions covering all aspects, right from consultation to project completion.
