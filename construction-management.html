Our Construction Management portfolio includes

  1. 1. Construction proposal and estimation support for EPC, EPCM and PMC
    • 1.1 Study of ITB requirement and listing out important data to be considered for proposal and estimation
    • 1.2 Preparation of construction driven schedule considering overall project schedule
    • 1.3 BOQ and SOR preparation from engineering input
    • 1.4 Plausibility check of BOQ quantities for optimization
    • 1.5 Market rate collection and parametric estimation
    • 1.6 Negotiation with probable sub-contractors to get the best optimized rate for proposal
    • 1.7 Calculation of power and water requirement for estimation and costing
    • 1.8 Calculation of development of temporary site facilities and its requirement based on project size
    • 1.9 Calculation of site infra cost for consideration in estimation
    • 1.10 Calculation of in house man hours required for H.O personnel for managing construction activities
    • 1.11 Calculation supervisory personnel requirement based on project schedule
    • 1.12 Calculation of direct labor and P&M requirement and its distribution for entire project duration
    • 1.13 Constructability study for proposal to get idea about the highest capacity of crane required and any other special requirement to be taken care in estimation
    • 1.14 Preparation of construction cost summary sheet for submission
  2. 2. Sub contract management support
    • 2.1 Support for Evaluation of site contractor
    • 2.2 Support for Sub contract negotiation and recommendation for award
    • 2.3 Support for Management of site contract awarded for execution
    • 2.4 Support for Settlement of extra work claim as per tender condition
    • 2.5 Support for Delay analysis and mitigation plan
    • 2.6 Support for Analysis for Liquidated damage and settlement
    • 2.7 Certification of site contractor bills
    • 2.8 Audit of bills
  3. 3. Construction Management support
    • 3.1 Support for Preparation of document for construction management requirement
    • 3.2 Support for Management of Recruitment of site engineers
    • 3.3 Support for Placement of site engineers as per requirement
    • 3.4 Support for Administrative management of all sites
    • 3.5 Monitoring of site progress and resource requirement
    • 3.6 Representing and conducting high level meeting with Client/Consultant
    • 3.7 Guidance for establishing effective material management at site
    • 3.8 Any other Construction Services specifically asked by client either In house / at site
  4. 4. QA/QC management support
    • 4.1 Support for Preparation of document for quality management requirement
    • 4.1 Support for Development of QAP, ITP, s and required formats for inspection
    • 4.1 Technical guidance for resolving complex quality issues
    • 4.1 Q M S Audits as per requirements of IS0 9001 - 2015
  5. 5. Construction Engineering support
    • 5.1 Detailed Constructability study and participation during 30% & 60% model review.
    • 5.2 Preparation of erection scheme for heavy lift and critical equipment
    • 5.3 Preparation of level 3,4,and 5 schedule
    • 5.4 Overall Guidance to Clients related to Construction of Industrial projects
  6. 6. Contract Management support
    • 6.1 Support for Negotiation with sub-contractor, Client /PMC for contract
    • 6.2 Support for Claim management
    • 6.3 Support for Settlement of extra order claim
    • 6.4 Techno Commercial Audits of site contractor's Invoices at site
  7. 7. Project Scheduling support
    • 7.1 Support for Preparation of project schedule L2,L3,L4 and L5
    • 7.2 Support for Preparation of billing schedule
    • 7.3 Support for Updation, delay analysis and mitigation plan
  8. 8. Budget and Finance support
    • 7.1 Budget control
    • 7.2 DCF evaluation
    • 7.3 Market Research
    • 7.3 Product Segmentation,Targeting and Promotion
    • 7.3 Dashboard Preparation
  9. 9. Tender process support
    • 7.1 Support for Preparation of full tender document
    • 7.2 Support for Preparation of condition of contract
    • 7.3 Support for Preparation of discipline wise scope of work and supply
    • 7.4 Support for Evaluation of bid received for technical bid analysis and commercial bid analysis
    • 7.5 Support for Recommendation for award of contract
    • 7.6 Audit / Review of Tender prepared by Others and providing suggestions for improvements
    • 7.7 Techno Commercial Audits of site contractors Invoices at site
