Our Project Management and Control portfolio includes

  1. 1. Project management support at proposal stage
    • 1.1 Preparing and providing Project Execution Plan
    • 1.2 Preparing and providing Project communication Plan
    • 1.3 Preparing and providing Project Coordination Plan or Manual
    • 1.4 Preparing and providing Project Quality Management Plan
    • 1.5 Preparing and providing Project Organization Chart
    • 1.6 Preparing and providing Project Change Management Procedure
    • 1.7 Preparing and providing Overall Project Schedule (L1 and L2 level)
    • 1.8 Preparing Concept documents for various activities at various stages during the Project Life.
  2. 2. Project management support during execution
    • 2.1 Preparing and providing Project Execution Plan
    • 2.2 Preparing and providing Project communication Plan
    • 2.3 Preparing and providing Project Coordination Plan or Manual
    • 2.4 Preparing Providing Project Quality Management Plan
    • 2.5 Preparing and Provide Project Organization Chart
    • 2.6 Preparing and Providing Project Change Management Procedure
    • 2.7 Preparing and providing Overall Project Schedule (L1 and L2 level)
    • 2.8 Review of the Monthly Progress Report and suggesting improvement actions
    • 2.9 Preparation of the Concept Note with process definition
    • 2.10 Analysing the process performance and recommending improvements with the new objectives
    • 2.11 Preparing WBS and Billing schedule
  3. 3. Contract Management Support
    • 3.1 Tender review and preparing pre-Bid queries
    • 3.2 Review of pre-Bid query response and preparing an impact report to be considered in the Proposal
    • 3.3 Preparing Kick off meeting (KOM) agenda and KOM Presentation
    • 3.4 Preparing delay analysis report with recommendation
    • 3.5 Preparing outgoing Contractual letters and preparing response to the incoming Contractual letters
    • 3.6 Preparing Project claim management Plan
    • 3.7 Scope review and providing Scope status with recommendation
    • 3.8 Conducting Risk Analysis and providing report with recommendation
  4. 4. Corporate Level Support
    • 4.1 Preparing PTR and company profile for the business development
  5. 5. Detail Engineering and Field Engineering Related Support
    • 5.1 Preparation of structural fabrication drawings
    • 5.2 BOM Preparation
    • 5.3 Preparation of Isometric Drawings for Site routed small bore lines
  6. 6. Site Support
    • 6.1 Preparing and providing of piping test pack
    • 6.2 Joint marking on isometric Drawings and preparing concept Note of History sheet
    • 6.3 Site visit for status review and providing recommendation for completion of balance work
  7. 7. Project Closure Support
    • 7.1 Time Extension and commercial close out recommendation
    • 7.2 Preparing and providing Contract closure documents
    • 7.3 Preparing Letters to client related to the Contract Closure.
